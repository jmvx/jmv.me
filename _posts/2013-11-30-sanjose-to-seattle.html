We decided to go on a road trip with friends for Thanksgiving Break. (San Jose, Portland, and Seattle.) A couple highlights I want to first mention (and are not pictured below) are:

  • Ground Kontrol: An awesome arcade bar. Tuesday nights are Rock Band night!
  • iPic Theaters: A movie theater where the seats are plush leather and you can drink a beer during the show.

Below are more highlights from our trip.

We stopped in Oregon for a hike. The path we took led us to an unexpected lonely beach by the ocean. There were a few people nearby bouldering on a large rock.

The apartment we stayed in while visiting Portland was right next to the Union Station. (Portland, Oregon)

We were invited to tour Xipiter headquarters. (Portland, Oregon)

The best burger in the world is at Brunch Box: “You Can Haz Cheeseburger” is a bacon cheeseburger in between two grilled cheese sandwiches. (Portland, Oregon)

We were invited to tour Simple headquarters while our friend (Principal of Xipiter) was picking up a package. (Portland, Oregon)

This is allegedly the reservoir that Blinky the three-eyed fish came from. (Oregon)

Halo statues in a Microsoft building. (Redmond, Washington)

We ate dinner at the rotating restaurant in the Space Needle. (Seattle, Washington)
