In November 2008, I participated in the Stony Brook Ballroom Competition. Below is a overview of how my partners and I placed in this competition. I danced with Florian Radu for Rhythm/Latin and Richard Rabin for Smooth/Standard.

  • 1st Place – Newcomer Standard Waltz
  • 2nd Place – Newcomer Smooth Waltz
  • 3rd Place – Newcomer Standard Tango
  • 3rd Place – Newcomer Smooth Tango
  • 5th Place – Newcomer Smooth Foxtrot
  • 5th Place – Newcomer Latin Rumba
  • 6th Place – Newcomer Rhythm Rumba

My partner Richard and I are in view at about 55 seconds in. We are in between two couples with red dresses. In case you still can’t find me, I do an underarm turn at 1:06. :)
