I am currently working on creating an iMessage bot that will answer queries about Caltrain arrival times. My initial design idea includes:

  • "next san jose north" will tell you when the next northbound trains from San Jose will be.
  • "last san francisco south" will tell you when the last train leaving San Francisco will be.

Eventually, I would also like it to specify whether the train arriving is a bullet or local train.
