In October 2008, I participated in the University of Connecticut Ballroom Competition. I received 4th place in Newcomer Rhythm Swing with my partner Samantha Avery.

Since almost everyone in the video is wearing a black dress like me, it is easier to find me by looking for my partner Samantha. She is wearing a white blouse.
